## Styling

At the moment, the wrapper exposes a CSS Custom Property for width: `--publishing-status--menu-items-container--width`  
It defaults to `280px`.

<Preview>
  <Story name="styling">
    <PublishingStatus.MenuItems
      style={{ "--publishing-status--menu-items-container--width": "560px" }}
    >
      <PublishingStatus.MessageContainer>
        <PublishingStatus.StatusView />
      </PublishingStatus.MessageContainer>
    </PublishingStatus.MenuItems>
  </Story>
</Preview>

Create your own container and provide it to the `*View` componenst via the `as` prop.  
You can also just not use this component
