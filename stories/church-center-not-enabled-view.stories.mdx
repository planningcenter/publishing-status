# ChurchCenterNotEnabledView

A view component with standard messaging for when Church Center is disabled.  
Customizable via `as` and `children`<super>\*</super> props.

<Preview>
  <Story name="default">
    <PublishingStatus.ChurchCenterNotEnabledView />
  </Story>
</Preview>

`children` can be customized but "Church Center is disabled." sentance is static.

## settingsURL

`settingsURL` reveals an "Update settings" link after the `children` message.

<Preview>
  <Story name="settingsURL">
    <PublishingStatus.ChurchCenterNotEnabledView settingsURL="#" />
  </Story>
</Preview>
